Philosophy

Pure Lash Mastery

Lashnique was created because we wanted lash treatments to feel different. We wanted them to feel considered. Slower. Quieter. Truly luxurious in the small details — the towels, the lighting, the music, the way you’re greeted at the door.

And we wanted the work itself to be undeniably excellent. Everything we do here begins with two ideas: that mastery comes from focus, and that luxury is just deep care, expressed.

What we specialise in

  • Wispy volume eyelash extensions
  • Eyelash extension infills (classic, hybrid, light volume, volume and angel)
  • Korean lash lift & tint
  • Safe, professional eyelash removals
  • Free patch tests for new clients

Our studio

The Lashnique studio is a private, by-appointment-only space tucked inside Clifton House on Merridale Road, Wolverhampton. You won’t share the room with another client. You won’t be rushed. You will be looked after.

Our approach

Every appointment begins with a proper consultation — eye shape, lash health, lifestyle, and the look you have in mind. From there we map a set designed entirely for you, working in a quiet, unhurried way that lets you genuinely rest.

Aftercare & longevity

Lash retention is mostly about what you do at home. Each client leaves with clear aftercare guidance, and we’ll always answer messages between appointments if anything comes up.
