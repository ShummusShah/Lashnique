Before You Visit

Consultation

Every first appointment includes a styling consultation. We’ll discuss your eye shape, lash health, lifestyle and the look you have in mind, then map a set that’s designed entirely for you.

Patch Test

If this is your first time with us, please book a complimentary patch test at least 48 hours before your appointment.

Aftercare

You’ll be sent home with clear aftercare guidance. Following it carefully is the secret to long-lasting retention.

Cancellations

We ask for at least 48 hours notice for any cancellations or rescheduling, as our appointment slots are in high demand.
